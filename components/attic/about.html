About

The invention of the telegraph in the 1830's and 40's marked the beginning of many changes in our world.

Electro-informational codes. The telegraph key completed a circuit which sent a current through a wire to an electro-magnet at the remote station. The electro-magnet made a series of marks on a paper strip as the current came and went. The pattern of long and short pulses of current and absence of current was used to encode the alphabet, the numbers, and other symbols.
